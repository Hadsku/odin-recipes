Very Simple dessert yet delicious

Ingredients:
  • 6 Apples
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Vanilla icecream
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°
  2. Remove the core of the apple without cutting it apart, there are some precise tools to do this
  3. Once the core is fully removed, add a knot of butter inside the apple, I usually fill the apple up to 4/5 with butter
  4. Fill the last 1/5 of your apple with brown sugar
  5. Put your apples in the oven for 15-20 minutes
  6. once your apples look done, take them out of the oven and they're ready to serve, add a notch of vanilla Ice cream and you're done !
