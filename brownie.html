Homemade Chocolate Brownie, extra T H I C C and best consumed with vanilla icecream

Ingredients:
  • 500g of Dark Chocolate
  • 300g of Salted Butter
  • 200g of Sugar
  • 180g of Flour
  • 6 whole Eggs
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°
  2. Break up your Chocolate and Butter into small chunks and melt them together in a Bain-Marie
  3. While your Chocolate and Butter are melting,in a big cooking bowl, mix your Eggs and Sugar together
  4. Once the Chocolate and the Butter has melted, allow them to cooldown before adding them to the Sugar/Eggs mixture as not to cook the Eggs
  5. Add the Chocolate/Butter to the Eggs/Sugar and mix them together, once it is homogeneous, add the Flour
  6. use a little notch of butter to butter the Plate before pouring the mixture in
  7. Put the plate into the Oven until you see that the middle has risen, you can use a knife to poke a hole into it to check how moist the inside is
  8. And Voila ! Your brownie is done !
